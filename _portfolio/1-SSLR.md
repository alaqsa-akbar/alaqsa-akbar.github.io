### Saudi Sign Language Recognition (SSLR)
*Aug 2025 – Jan 2026*  
- A research project under Dr. Hamzah Luqman to investigate and develop high quality sign language recognition models on Saudi Sign Language (SSL) through the Isharah-500 dataset
- Achieved WER on the dev set of 6.74%
